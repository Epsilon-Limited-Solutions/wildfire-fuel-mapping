Satellite Change Detection (2020-2022)

What satellites detected:

  • 25.6% of area showed high vegetation stress
  • 47.6% of area showed moderate stress
  • NDVI decreased (vegetation loss)
  • NDMI decreased (moisture deficit)
  • NBR decreased (fuel accumulation)

Key insight: Satellites detected fuel changes that LANDFIRE's static 2020 map couldn't capture.
